Presto makes sharing ideas anonymous, whilst still allowing their authors to take full credit.

The presto process goes like this:
  • Create a presto ✅
  • Send invites to your team members 📧
  • Upload your ideas (anonymously) 🕵️
  • Select the best ideas ❤️
  • Their authors can reveal themselves! 🥳
